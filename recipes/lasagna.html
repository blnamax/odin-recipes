Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and melted cheese. It's perfect to share with family and friends.

This recipe is easy to follow a delicious, ideal for beginners and experoenced cooks alike.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 lb groun beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup grates Paremsan cheese
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with salt and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta mixture, ground beef, and mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  8. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top.
  9. Bake for 25–30 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.
