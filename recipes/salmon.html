Grilled Salmon with Lemon Garlic Sauce

Description

This grilled salmon is a healthy and elegant dish packed with protein and omega-3 fatty acids. It features a fresh lemon garlic sauce that enhances the natural flavor of the fish.

Perfect for a light dinner or a special occasion, this recipe is simple, nutritious, and beautifully presented.

Ingredientes

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
    2. Brush the salmon fillets with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
    3. Grill the salmon for 4â€“5 minutes per side until cooked through.
    4. In a small bowl, mix garlic, lemon juice, and lemon zest.
    5. Drizzle the lemon garlic sauce over the grilled salmon.
    6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
