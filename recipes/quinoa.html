Quinoa Salad

Description

This quinoa salad is a healthy and elegant dish, packed with protein, fresh vegetables, and creamy avocado. Perfect for lunch or a light dinner, it’s colorful, flavorful, and easy to prepare.

It’s ideal for anyone looking for a nutritious meal that’s both satisfying and visually appealing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 cup roasted cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup roasted bell peppers
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse quinoa and cook in water or broth according to package instructions.
  2. Roast cherry tomatoes and bell peppers with olive oil until tender.
  3. Let quinoa cool slightly, then combine with roasted vegetables.
  4. Add diced avocado, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Mix gently and serve immediately or chilled.
